About Unlimited Questions

Our Unlimited Questions feature allows you to generate as many questions as you need from any document. Unlike traditional systems that provide a limited set of questions, our AI-powered system can create endless variations based on the content of your uploaded document.

Key Benefits

  • Endless Practice: Never run out of questions for studying or testing
  • Smart Generation: AI creates unique questions based on document content
  • Continuous Learning: Generate new questions anytime you need more practice
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Cover all topics and concepts from your document
  • Time-Saving: No need to manually create additional questions

How It Works

Our system analyzes your document and identifies key concepts, definitions, processes, and relationships. When you click the "More Questions" button, it generates additional questions using different aspects of the same content, ensuring variety while maintaining relevance.

Try It Yourself

Upload any document and use the "More Questions" button to see unlimited question generation in action. Each click generates 10 new questions based on your specific document content.
