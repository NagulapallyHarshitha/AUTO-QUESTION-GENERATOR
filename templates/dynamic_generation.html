Dynamic Generation

Intelligent question generation that adapts to your content

About Dynamic Generation

Our Dynamic Generation feature uses advanced AI algorithms to intelligently analyze your document and create questions that are perfectly tailored to your specific content. The system adapts to different document types, topics, and complexity levels.

Key Benefits

  • Smart Adaptation: Automatically adjusts to different document types and topics
  • Context-Aware: Understands the context and relationships within your content
  • Multi-Level Analysis: Analyzes definitions, processes, comparisons, and applications
  • Concept Mapping: Identifies key concepts and their relationships
  • Instant Processing: Generates questions in real-time as you need them

How Dynamic Generation Works

1

Content Analysis

The system reads your document and identifies key elements like definitions, processes, comparisons, and applications.

2

Concept Extraction

Important concepts and terms are extracted and categorized based on their significance and frequency.

3

Question Formulation

AI generates questions using appropriate templates based on the content type and difficulty level.

4

Quality Assurance

Each question is validated to ensure it's relevant, clear, and accurately represents the document content.
