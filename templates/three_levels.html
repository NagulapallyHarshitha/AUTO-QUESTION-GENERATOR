About Three Learning Levels

Our Three Learning Levels system provides tailored questioning experiences for users at different stages of learning. Whether you're a beginner seeking basic understanding or an expert looking for deep analysis, we have the right level for you.

Basic Level
Foundational

Perfect for: Beginners, quick reviews, and basic comprehension testing

  • Simple factual questions
  • Key concept identification
  • Basic understanding assessment
  • Straightforward multiple-choice format
  • Immediate feedback with explanations
Medium Level
Comprehensive

Perfect for: Intermediate learners, detailed study, and application testing

  • Definition-based questions
  • Process and method understanding
  • Application scenario questions
  • Moderate complexity challenges
  • Detailed explanatory feedback
Advanced Level
Expert

Perfect for: Experts, critical thinkers, and deep analysis

  • Comparative analysis questions
  • Real-world application scenarios
  • Advantage/disadvantage evaluation
  • Complex problem-solving
  • In-depth analytical feedback

1. Start with Basic: If you're new to the topic, begin with basic questions to build foundation
